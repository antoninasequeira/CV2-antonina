Antonina Sequeira

Regional Director of One Stop Search

One Stop Search is currently managed at a regional level, by Antonina Sequeira. One Stop Search focuses on connecting SMEs with the procurement department of Multinationals and connecting them with regional markets. The two pillars of One Stop Search are to educate SMEs in business skills and then connect them with possible buyers. Antonina’s network is broad, and varies from high government officials, multinationals CEOs and Procurement Managers, to more than 1246 SMEs in Central America. Her experience in working in the Nicaraguan and Costa Rican Market has given her a high profile in gender empowerment and Leadership topics. Has worked closely with the Vice President of Costa Rica and with the Ministry of Commerce and Economics. Holds connections with large successful local companies both in Costa Rica and Nicaragua. Graduated in University of Texas at Austin, and studied at the Institute de Touraine in France, lived abroad many years, thus being a multicultural person and a firm believer in collaborative leadership in the work environment.Promerica

Antonina Sequeira counts with more than 15 years of experience in marketing, regional commerce, as an entrepreneur and has worked directly with NGOs and SMEs. Director of WEConnect International in Costa Rica during four years, she generated a series of strategies with the purpose to strengthen the niche of Women SMEs in the market and position the brand name in the country. Her representation of the international program, the Red Shoe Movement in 2016, gave her the opportunity to grow in the development of Women’s empowerment in the workforce and leadership skills.WEConnect

Antonina Sequeira is part of the Board of Directors of “Movimiento Balance” initiative focused on gender equality and co-founded by the possible future first lady of Costa Rica. In Nicaragua she has been able to create alliances with Ernst and Young and Incae Business School.
